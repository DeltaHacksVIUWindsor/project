Our Mission

To connect the less fortunate with the resources and services they need to turn their life around, and end poverty altogether



Our Plan of Self-Destruction

While it may seem silly, our objective is to provide a service that is so successful, that our users will eventually no longer need it. As such, we hope that down the line such a service will not be necessary, and all may be able to live in a sustainable manner.
